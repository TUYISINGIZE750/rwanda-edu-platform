## ğŸ¨ Brand Guidelines

### When to Use Gradients
- Headers and navigation bars
- Primary buttons and CTAs
- Hero sections
- Card highlights
- Text emphasis (headings)

### Typography
- **Headings**: Bold, often with gradient
- **Body**: Regular weight, high readability
- **Buttons**: Semibold to bold
- **Captions**: Smaller, medium weight

### Spacing
- Generous padding for breathing room
- Consistent gap sizes (4, 6, 8 units)
- Rounded corners (xl, 2xl, 3xl)
